About IT Wonders

IT Wonders established in Singapore in year 2014 then in Malaysia in year 2015 with a simple intention to help businesses, enterprises and corporates to enhance their online branding and create more values to their customer through providing customized web design and development services.

IT Wonders provided me a good learning platform to let me explore to the lastest technology and get familiar with latest developement trends. As a beginner to web industry, what I know about web developement was basic HTML, CSS and Javascript before I join IT Wonders, I really learned a lot in IT Wonders and I will take this as a very good initial to continue my web developer career in the future.

The working environment here is very comfortable for the internship student since you will get a lot of changes to explore yourself to the new thing that you may never heared in your University.
