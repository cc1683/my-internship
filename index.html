About IT Wonders

IT Wonders established in Singapore in year 2014 then in Malaysia in year 2015 with a simple intention to help businesses and corporates to enhance their online branding and create more values to their customer through providing customized web development services.
IT Wonders provided me a good learning platform to let me explore to the latest technology and get familiar with latest developement trends. I learned a lot of new things in IT Wonders and I will take this as a very good initial to continue my web developer career in the future.
The working environment here is very comfortable for the internship student since you will get a lot of chances to explore yourself to the new thing that you may never heared in your University.

Capchavate

Capchavate is the first project that I get involved in IT Wonders. It's an online pinboard system for inspiration of foods and desserts. It's build by the PHP Laravel framework as the back-end and Vue as the front-end. My task is focusing on the front-end of this project. I modify the layout and makesure its works on difference size of devices. I also get the chances to work with the databases however it's too difficult to me :( fortunately, I still able to finish the task with the help of my colleagues.
